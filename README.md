# Zoteboat
Welcome to text-based Zoteboat: A Sailing Adventure

--- Originally a computer science assignment, this project goes above and beyond the scope of the project, and as I am having fun I will continue to develop it ---

In this game, you are Zote the Mighty. Starting from humble beginnings, you take your boat and set sail with your trusty nail and fishing rod. 
As you head off into distant horizons, numerous challenges will arise such as ravenous monsters, conniving NPCs, and above all, STARVATION. 
Battle your way across the waves to fish, find distant lands of treasure and claim all the glory for yourself.

Good luck!

---
This is a console text-based game. The user will enter commands from the following list and the game will tell you your story.

Current commmands:
Sail
Eat [food] - WIP
Fish (When bubbles)
Talk (npc) - WIP
Trade (npc) - WIP
Fight (enemy) - WIP
Inventory
Look
Drop - WIP
Turns
Help [command]
Restart
Exit (quit)
